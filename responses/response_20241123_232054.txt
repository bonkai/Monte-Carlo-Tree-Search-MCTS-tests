Question: if you put an orange in the trash then take the bag out of the trash and take the bag to the dumb and put a new bad in the trash where is the orange?

Answer:
If you put an orange in the trash, took the trash bag out, and then took that bag to the dumpster, the orange would be inside the trash bag in the dumpster. When you put a new trash bag in your trash can, it does not affect the contents of the dumpster. Therefore, the orange is in the dumpster.

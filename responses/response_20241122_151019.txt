Question: my kitchen has a garbage bag in the can like normal but it's full of garbage. i threw an orange in the trash before i got the bag out and took that bag to the dump to get rid of it. then i came home and put a new trash bag in the can. where is the orange now?

Answer:
Let's review the sequence of events:

1. You had a full garbage bag in the can.
2. You threw an **orange** into the trash, but you didn't take out the existing bag at that time (you put the new trash bag in later).
3. Before taking out the old bag and putting it in the dumpster, you took out the full garbage bag from the can to put it in the dumpster.
4. You then emptied the contents of the full garbage bag into a new bag and took it to the dump.

So... **the orange was thrown into the original garbage bag before you took out the old bag**!

After taking out the old bag, you would have seen that **it's now empty**, since the orange had already been placed in the trash. The full garbage bag went into the dumpster, leaving behind an empty bag.

But... where did you put the new bag with the orange inside? You said you came home and put a new trash bag in the can, which means the original bag (with the orange) is now **back in the kitchen**!

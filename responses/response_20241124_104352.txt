Question: greg and tammy have the same number of brothers and sisters. they each have 5 sisters and 4 brothers. and each of their siblings has the same number of siblings as eachother. how many girls and boys are in each house?

Answer:
Let's analyze the information:

**Common Information for Both Greg and Tammy:**

* Each has 5 sisters.
* Each has 4 brothers.

Since they have the same number of brothers and sisters, and we know the exact counts:

* **Girls in Each House:** 5 (sisters) + 1 (either Greg or Tammy, but since the question asks for girls, we only count Tammy in her house and no one additional in Greg's house as he is a boy) 
	+ For Tammy's house: 5 sisters + Tammy = **6 Girls**
	+ For Greg's house: Just the 5 sisters = **5 Girls**
* **Boys in Each House:** 4 (brothers) + 1 (either Greg or Tammy, but since we're counting boys now, we only count Greg in his house and no one additional in Tammy's house as she is a girl)
	+ For Greg's house: 4 brothers + Greg = **5 Boys**
	+ For Tammy's house: Just the 4 brothers = **4 Boys**

**Each House's Breakdown:**

* **Tammy's House:**
	+ Girls: **6** (5 sisters + Tammy)
	+ Boys: **4**
* **Greg's House:**
	+ Girls: **5** (just the sisters)
	+ Boys: **5** (4 brothers + Greg)
